
# 📚 Scholarmatch

**Scholarmatch** is a modern platform designed to *make scholarship applications easier* and more accessible for students. It helps users discover, track, and apply to scholarships, reducing the burden of manual search and organization.

## 🧠 About

Scholarmatch aims to simplify the scholarship application process by aggregating opportunities tailored to each student and matching them with opportunities that actually fit. Whether you’re a student looking for financial aid or a mentor helping students succeed, Scholarmatch provides tools to make scholarship discovery effortless.

---

## ⭐ Features

- 🔍 **Personalized Scholarship Discovery** – Browse and filter available scholarship opportunities across Canada.
- 📝 **Application Tracker** – Track scholarship deadlines and submission statuses.
- 📌 **Personalized Recommendations** – Get recommendations tailored to your profile.
- 📊 **Dashboard Analytics** – Visualize your application progress and what next steps you can take to strengthen your profile.
